ALBERT REDESIGNING PROJECT

BY TIYA WANG

Problem Statement

The current course search process for NYU students through Albert has proved itself to be unorganized and inefficient for its users. We seek to redesign this system to make it more user friendly.

Research

Survey

We created a google form to ask NYU students' thought about Albert. We collected 41 samples in total.

1. Rate your overall satisfaction.



2. Whether or not you use the search bar feature.



3. Whether or not you use the filter feature.



4. Preferred Methods of Searching for a Specific Course



5. Your way to find a course.



Mind Map

According to the mind map, we identified the MVPs:

  • Search Features
  • Filters
  • Information Organization
  • Ability to Enroll
  • Fit on Screen
