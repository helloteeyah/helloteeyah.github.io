ABOUT TIME REDESIGNING MOBILE APP

BY TIYA WANG


Brand Overview

About Time Coffee is a boutique chain with multiple locations throughout Manhattan. Known for its straightforward menu, affordable prices, stylish canned packaging, rewarding loyalty program, and distinctive flavors, About Time Coffee has tailored a unique experience for New Yorkers.

Problem Statement

Users of the "About Time Coffee" mobile app are experiencing frustration and inefficiency due to the lack of a clear and accessible ordering function. The hidden nature of the order tracking feature results in a significant time investment from users to locate. This design flaw not only impacts customer satisfaction but also potentially affects the frequency of orders and overall user engagement with the app. In order to improve customer satisfaction, I decided to redesign a clear, efficient, and streamlined ordering process for the app.

Research

User Data

I have collected user data from a survey to inquire about their experiences with the mobile app. I selected the most significant questions that highlight the issues:

- Have you used the order tracking feature within the app? If so, how would you describe your experience?



- Please rate your overall satisfaction with the current ordering process in the app.



- Regarding ordering coffee, how important are the following features to you? Please rate each one.




The user trend analysis from all the questions:

- Order Frequency: A large proportion of users are regular customers, ordering several times a week, suggesting that they would benefit from a streamlined ordering process.

- Satisfaction Levels: There is room for improvement, with neutral to dissatisfied feelings being common, indicating that enhancements could significantly impact user satisfaction.

- Ease of Ordering: The process seems to be of average ease, with some users finding it difficult. A simplified ordering process could be beneficial.

- Order Tracking Feature: There are mixed feelings about this feature, with a notable portion of users having a negative experience or not using it at all, suggesting that this feature needs to be more intuitive and prominent.

- Important Features: Real-time order tracking and seeing estimated pick-up times are considered very important by a majority, followed by the importance of quick reorders and drink customization.

- App Navigation: The current navigation receives mixed reviews, but leans more towards being intuitive. Still, there is a notable group that finds it not intuitive, suggesting a need for a more user-friendly interface.

- Checkout Process Preference: A clear preference for a single-page checkout indicates that users value efficiency and simplicity.

- Visual Design Impact: Most users find that visual design enhances their experience, meaning aesthetic improvements could contribute positively to user satisfaction.

- Comparative Performance: Many users feel the app is about the same as other coffee apps, indicating there's potential to outshine competitors with targeted improvements.

    • Unable to edit when in the cart.

    Based on the user trends and the comparative analysis, I identified the MVPs:
    • Provides an estimated pickup time.
    • Displays order status on the main page (placed, preparing, ready).
    • Offers the ability to reorder previous items.
    • Offers the ability to edit in the cart.
    • Enables review of the location before checkout.

    Reflection

    User Review

    The general checkout process is very smooth, and the visuals are quite pleasing. However, the back button is difficult to click. Perhaps adding a transparent mask behind it could increase the clickable area. Additionally, since there are many overlays, it's important to reconsider the rationale behind this design choice for progressing to the next steps. Also, consider the methods for navigating within the overlays: whether to click a button or slide down to proceed to the next step, and whether to click outside or add an 'X' button to close the overlay.

    What will I do in the future?

    In the future, I plan to add more components and variants. Currently, some buttons are not clickable, but I will work on completing their functionality to ensure users have a comprehensive experience. Additionally, I need to give more thought to the transitions between each step. Also, I will conduct further user testing.
